Tips for better results

→

Be specific in the fields

The more details you provide (frameworks, versions, objectives), the more accurate the result generated by the AI ​​will be.

→

Iterate and refine

Use the first result as a basis and ask the AI ​​to adjust specific parts. "Add more details in the commands section" works fine.

→

Review before use

Always review the generated file. Adjust paths, commands, and URLs to exactly match your project.

→

Keep updated

Regenerate the file when your project evolves significantly. An outdated CLAUDE.md can confuse the agent.
